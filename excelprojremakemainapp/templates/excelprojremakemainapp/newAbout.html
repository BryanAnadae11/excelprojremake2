About the Company

More About Alpha Oil

Alpha Oil is a multi-disciplinary energy company delivering integrated oil and gas solutions across upstream, offshore, and midstream operations. We provide comprehensive and customized services by combining technical expertise, advanced engineering, and operational excellence across the global energy sector.
With deep experience in exploration, field development, offshore production systems, FPSO operations, logistics, and energy trading, we support national oil companies, independent producers, and industrial clients worldwide.
At Alpha Oil, we recognize the critical role oil and gas continues to play in the global economy. Our mission is to responsibly unlock energy resources while mitigating risk, improving efficiency, and adhering to the highest standards of safety, environmental protection, and regulatory compliance.
Alpha Oil operates across Europe, Africa, the Middle East, America, Australia, Saudi Arabia and offshore basins worldwide, with partnerships and regulated operations in multiple jurisdictions. Our team brings strong expertise in petroleum engineering, offshore operations, project finance, logistics, and energy markets.
We are committed to building a safe, efficient, and resilient operational ecosystem that enables reliable production, transportation, and delivery of energy resources. Through advanced technologies, real-time monitoring, and disciplined project execution, we make complex energy operations manageable and dependable.
Since inception, our goal has been to become an industry leader in offshore operations, field development, and integrated energy services — defined by performance, safety culture, and client trust. Our operational approach combines engineering discipline, risk management, and commercial insight to deliver sustainable long-term value.

Our Experience

Established in 2011, Alpha Oil has grown into a global energy company specializing in offshore production, upstream development, and integrated oil & gas services. Our success is built on operational reliability, technical excellence, and long-term partnerships.

We manage and operate energy assets for national oil companies, independent producers, industrial consumers, and trading partners worldwide. Our organization consists of 500+ professionals, including petroleum engineers, offshore specialists, project managers, and commercial experts.

We maintain operational and commercial presence in London, Geneva, Abu Dhabi, Singapore, Jeddah, Melbourne, and offshore production regions. Our leadership team brings decades of experience across upstream development, FPSO operations, subsea systems, logistics, and energy trading.

Through strategic partnerships with engineering firms, offshore contractors, logistics providers, and technology partners, we continuously enhance our operational capabilities. As we look to the future, we remain committed to innovation, digitalization, and responsible energy development to support global demand.
